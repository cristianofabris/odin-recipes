Risotto

Description

Mushrooms risotto is a traditional Italian dish, creamy and flavorful, prepared with rice, mushrooms, broth, and cheese.

Ingredients for 4 servings

  • 320g Carnaroli rice
  • 400g mixed mushrooms (porcini, etc.)
  • 1 liter vegetable broth
  • 1 onion
  • 50g butter
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Clean the mushrooms and slice them.
  2. Finely chop the onion and sauté it in a pan with the olive oil.
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook over medium heat for 5-7 minutes.
  4. In a large pot, toast the rice dry for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the white wine and let it evaporate.
  6. Add one ladle of hot broth at a time, stirring continuously.
  7. After about 10 minutes, add the sautéed mushrooms to the rice.
  8. Continue cooking, adding broth as needed, for another 8-10 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat and stir in butter and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Adjust salt and pepper, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve.
