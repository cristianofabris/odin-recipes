Pasta al pomodoro

Description

Pasta al Pomodoro is a simple Italian dish featuring pasta in a fresh tomato sauce. It's a staple of Italian cuisine, loved for its simplicity and flavors.

Ingredients for 4 servings

  • 400g spaghetti or any pasta of your choice
  • 400g canned whole peeled tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 5-6 fresh basil leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. If using fresh tomatoes, blanch them in boiling water, peel, and chop. If using canned tomatoes, crush them with your hands.
  2. In a large pan, gently heat the olive oil and add the peeled, lightly crushed garlic cloves.
  3. When the garlic turns golden, add the tomatoes and a pinch of salt.
  4. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  6. Remove the garlic cloves from the sauce and add torn basil leaves.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the sauce.
  8. Toss the pasta with the sauce over medium heat for a minute to combine thoroughly.
  9. Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese and extra basil leaves.
