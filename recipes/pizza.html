Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a flat base of leavened dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients.

ingredients for 4 Pizzas

  • 1kg of flour
  • 600ml of water
  • 2g of active dry yeast
  • 15g of salt
  • 250g of tomato sauce
  • 250g of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, water, yeast, and salt until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise at room temperature for 8-24 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 equal portions and shape into balls.
  5. Let the dough balls rest for 1-2 hours at room temperature.
  6. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (ideally 250-300Â°C) with a pizza stone or steel inside.
  7. Stretch each dough ball into a thin round.
  8. Top each pizza with a thin layer of tomato sauce and small pieces of mozzarella.
  9. Bake for 5-10 minutes, or until the crust is charred and the cheese is melted.
